gasr Backstory

Me, EggTank, rguzgasr, or whatever in hell will you call me, am opening this log sort of thing to give a backstory of gasr and include all details if possible.

It is October 30, 2022 as I write this.

'goo animal simulator: reborn', formerly known as 'gasr', as the name suggests, is the remake of the game "goo animal simulator" made by Rufus14. This game, both, are based on 'Changed' and 'The Culling', the first being a furry fetish game, second being survival games ported to a fps game.

I liked 'gas' very much since I had something for 'Changed'. It was all fun and games until Rufus14 shut 'gas' down. That was a sort of breakpoint for me since 'gas' was the only reason why I was on roblox. Sort of.

The date is: 12/03/2021.

I decided to learn some Luau to remake 'gas'.

Development Team

"Panzer Studios". At first, we made a so called 'development team' that was me, vio and shit. They were no use.

Development Progress

The "Panzer Studios" team started to remake 'goo animal simulator'. The lead developer was me, myself and I. As from what I remember, the first thing to be developed was the heads-up display. 'We' made the lockers afterwards. The lasers after that, puddles and doors. All of this were extremely unorganized. I had no idea what- Oh, sorry. 'We' had no idea what 'we' were doing. I remember using values as remote events, jesus. That was all horrible. Glad those days are over.

The date is: 12/12/2021.

Development Team

"Panzer Studios" Me, Vio

Development Progress

'We' were making some sort of shop thing. Vio begged about it, the shopkeepers were Vio and Janitor. I remember doing a lot of shit to make animations work.

The date is: 12/24/2021.

Development Team

Me, Vio

Development Progress

Vio does something for the sake of the game for the first time: making infected faces. 'We' were making infected playermodels. As far as I know form the discord, we made lockers too. This was how the game looked.

The date is: 12/25/2021.

"the reason why i liked ga is because of the blackouts" -Me

Development Team

Development Progress

Vio is doing nothing but to spam so called 'Fumo Plushies' everywhere.

The date is: 12/16/2021

Development Team

Development Progress

"gotta script fists now" Oh boy, what a mistake that was. The classic "Oh no! Our table, it's broken!" meme was being made.

Cut to the chase. The cancellation of gasr.(08/16/2022)

Why, and how?

Quote "tomorrow decides gasr's fate. if i can recover the game then good news. else, game is cancelled and i will not come back to it ever again, i've already spent a lot of my life and screwed my school for this game. im bored. i had enough. i want to live freely without the thought of adding and fixing crap for gasr. understand or not. im at my limit." unquote.

I was developing gasr as usual. All of a sudden, no soul knows how, the file got corrupted. Everything. That's all, I guess. The blockquote explains it all.

What now?

It was cancelled.

The birth of gasr:lite

Thankfully I devlogged this game.

gasr:lite devlog #1 10/08/2022

I miss gasr. I know you too. Who doesn't. So, here's the deal. I rewrite gasr. From scratch. Organized. Knowing what I do this time. And, this remaster will be named: gasr:rewritten

No bullshit this time. I know luau from top to bottom. I will make everything on my own. Like the name suggests, I will rewrite everything. Right hand on god. Learning from my mistakes, I will make the map first. With snap to grid fixed at a stud. That will make the map better in every way.

After making a rough map, I will start decorating it. I plan to rip the main generator from gasr. That didn't get corrupted, at last.

Will try to keep the rewrite as close to the original gas. Raytracing included.

After the map, I will start combat. With CFrame manipulation. The weapons are ready. Scripts will be rewritten too.

Transfurmations at last. Playermodels are presumed to be the same. I put infections to the last stage since- uhh, thinking about it now, I should make transfurs first. That way, the combat can be adapted for infected easily.

I am self confident this time. THIS GAME WILL BE SERVED TO THE CHANGED COMMUNITY NO MATTER WHAT.

gasr:lite devlog #2 10/13/2022

I completed the map in 2 days(4 hours). Only walls and floors. I will start decorating the map today. It will presumably take 6 hours, which is roughly 2 days worth of free time. Making the map first was a good idea.

I still haven't decided what to do after the map. I presume combat. Eh, better finish the map first.

gasr:lite devlog #3 10/14/2022

Part 1

We have doors now. Lockable and gibbing. I wasted one of my audio rights for audio that already exists on the marketplace. I should search the marketplace more. It's gonna be weekend so some serious advancements will be made, I hope.

Part 2

It's afternoon. The teacher isn't here so, this gives me some time to think about what should I do for this game. Map is smaller than what I thought. I am trying to make this game as similar to that one gas animation i made a loooong time ago. I mean, lights flicker when the generator is under attack, lockable doors, having to start the generator in spawn to enable lasers etc. I spent yesterday making doors, and there is one problem to fix. When you lock an open door, it closes first. And, when you get stuck under a door, you explode. When you explode, the door gets into an alarm state and opens. This causes the door to get locked wile open. I have to fix that. 2 hours and 12 minutes left until school ends. I don't know what to do. I'm bored as fuck. Sleepy too. I can't think of anything. I think I will end the log here.

I made a trailer, got 120ish views so far, that's good, amen. Official beta release was yesterday, it functions better than expected. There are still a lot of bugs to fix. Actually, not much. I have to fix this one bug that breaks the game entirely after getting stunned. The raycast is goofy too. Have to fix these and we will have a stable version.

I understand everything now, why everyone made alternative gasses. Some random group of people leaked the gas engine, so everyone published the leak and made some bank. One of those guys commented on the trailer, and I asked them if i could use the engine for reference purposes only.

Fuck you, "BSD". You think you did a fine job with that "gasr" of yours, but you used the fucking leaked engine. Kill yourself.

AFAIK, the combat folder of the person gets deleted for a reason. There is abso-fucking-lutely no script that will delete the combat folder. I suspect one thing. The weapons. The main script might be deleting the combat folder. It's the only blameable script, you know. I'M curious as hell now. Gotta get home and figure it out.

Oh fuck. Oh shit. I-I don't know anymore. Just remembered the combat folder doesn't restore after respawning. What dumb son of a bitch is causing this, what the fuck? Now I'M extra fucking curious. God help me.

Lets brainstorm for a bit. This week will be spent on bug fixes and stabilization. If I can stabilize the game in a short period of time, I will start making the items and weapons. Weapon base is already ready, I have to make an item base. The rest is copy paste. It will be awesome if I were to fully release the game before 2023.
